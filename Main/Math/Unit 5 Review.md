$$

$$

$$
\begin{align}
c^2=a ^2 - b ^2 -2ab\cos C \\
A = \cos^{-1}\left( \frac{b^2+c^2-a^2}{2bc} \right)
\end{align}
$$

$$

\end{align}

$$
\begin{align}
y = 2x + 3 \\
r\sin \theta = 2(r\cos \theta)+3 \\
\sin \theta = 2 \cos \theta+3 \\
\sin \theta - 2\cos \theta = 3
\end{align}
$$

## Polar Graphs

### Circles
- $r = a\cos \theta$ 
	- $a>0$ right of y axis
	- $a<0$ left of y axis
	- polar axis symmetry
- $r=a\sin \theta$
	- $a>0$ above polar axis
	- $a<0$ below polar axis
	- $\theta =\frac{\pi}{2}$
	- y axis symmetry
### Roses
- $r=a\sin(n\theta)$ 
	- $\theta = \frac{\pi}{2}$ symmetry
	- 1st petal at $\theta = \frac{\pi}{2}$
- $r=a\cos(n\theta)$
	- polar axis symmetry
	- 1st petal at $\theta = 0$
- N is odd = N petals
- N is even = 2N petals
### Limaçons
- $r = a+b\cos \theta$
	- polar axis symmetry
- $r= a+b\sin \theta$
	- $\frac{\pi}{2}$ symmetry
- $|a|<|b|$ = loop
- $|a|=|b|$ = cartoid
- $|a|>|b|$ = dent/dimpled limaçon/clam
- Max Radius = $|a|+|b|$
- Min Radius = $|a|-|b|$
